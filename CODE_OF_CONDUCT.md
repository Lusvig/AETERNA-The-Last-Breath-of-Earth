# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

AETERNA is a project born from the desire to explore humanity's deepest fears and greatest hopes. Our community shares a commitment to creating something meaningful and respectful.

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. Regardless of background, experience level, or personal characteristics, everyone is valued.

## Expected Behavior

### Do
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Listen with intention to other perspectives
- Give and receive constructive feedback graciously
- Focus on collaboration and shared learning
- Respect others' time and effort
- Help others grow and improve their skills
- Acknowledge mistakes and learn from them

### Don't
- Use language that is discriminatory, harassing, or hostile
- Engage in personal attacks or trolling
- Spam or flood channels with unwanted content
- Claim credit for others' work
- Violate intellectual property or licensing
- Engage in harassment or discrimination
- Post without reviewing community guidelines first

## Our Standards

### Unacceptable Behavior

Violations include but are not limited to:
- Harassment, bullying, or discrimination
- Sexual harassment or unwanted advances
- Slurs, dehumanizing language, or hate speech
- Threats of violence or self-harm
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Spam or commercial solicitation
- Persistent disruption of discussions

### Consequences

Violations will result in:
- Warning and request to modify behavior
- Temporary muting or suspension
- Permanent ban if severe or repeated

## Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation:
1. Report to: conduct@aeterna-game.dev
2. Include: Description, dates, participants (if known)
3. We will respond within 48 hours
4. All reports are confidential

## Our Responsibility

Community maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying what constitutes unacceptable behavior
- Taking swift action on violations
- Protecting reporter confidentiality
- Modifying or removing content that violates guidelines
- Providing fair and consistent enforcement

## Scope

This code applies to:
- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Discord server and channels
- Email communications
- In-game interactions
- Any official community spaces

## Appreciation

Thank you for helping us build a community worthy of the story we're telling.
